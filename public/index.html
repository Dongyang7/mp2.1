OVERWATCH

Overwatch is a team-based multiplayer first-person shooter video game developed and published by Blizzard Entertainment. Here is the plot of the game:

In a time of global crisis, an international task force of heroes banded together to restore peace to a war-torn world. This organization is known as Overwatch. After many years, Overwatch's influence waned and it was eventually disbanded. However, Overwatch may be gone, the world still needs heroes.

Offense characters have high mobility and are known for their ability to deal large amounts of damage. To balance this, offense characters have a low number of hit points.
Defense characters excel at protecting specific locations and creating choke points. They can also provide several means of field support, such as sentry set-up and trapping the enemy team.
Tank characters have the most hit points out of all the characters in the game. Because of this, they are able to draw enemy fire away from their teammates to themselves, so as to disrupt the enemy team.
Support characters are utility characters that have abilities that enhance their own team and/or weaken the enemy team. Their buffs and debuffs they provide ensure that their teammates who do will make short work of their opponents.

MAPS

  1. Assault Mode: The attacking team is tasked with capturing two target points in sequence on the map, while the defending team must stop them.
  2. Escort Mode: The attacking team is tasked with escorting a payload to a certain delivery point before time runs out, while the defending team must stop them.
  3. Hybrid Mode (Assault/Escort): The attacking team has to capture the payload and escort it to its destination, while the defending team tries to hold them back.
  4. Control Mode: Each team tries to capture and maintain a common control point until their capture percentage reaches 100%. This game mode is played in a best-of-three format.
